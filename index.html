Our Mission

At Lucky Girl Who Win, we believe in the transformative power of technology to empower Afghan girls and women.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide access to quality education, resources, and opportunities that enable them to succeed in their careers and personal lives.

Our Mission

We envision a future where Afghan girls and women are leaders in technology, driving innovation and positive change in their communities.

Our Programs

Coding Education

Provide coding and technology education to Afghan girls.

Every girl deserves the opportunity to learn and thrive in the digital world.

Education is the key to unlocking potential and creating opportunities for development and achieving their goals.

Mentorship

Offering mentorship and support to help Afghan girls achieve their goals.

Mentors provide guidance, support, and valuable insights to help girls navigate their coding journey.

It helps girls build the skills, confidence, and resilience to succeed in the tech industry and beyond.

Community Building

Creating a community of empowered women who can support and inspire each other.

Provides a safe and supportive space for Afghan girls to connect, share, and learn from each other.

Through online forums, and social events, girls can build relationships, seek advice , and find inspiration.
